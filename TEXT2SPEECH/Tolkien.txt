He was the author of the classic high fantasy works The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings.
He served as the Rawlinson and Bosworth Professor of Anglo-Saxon and
Fellow of Pembroke College, Oxford, from 1925 to 1945 and
Merton Professor of English Language and Literature and 
Fellow of Merton College, Oxford, from 1945 to 1959.
He was at one time a close friend of C. S. Lewisthey were both members of the informal literary discussion group known as the Inklings.
